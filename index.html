About LoopMeets

LoopMeets is a traveler-matching platform designed to make solo travel social, safe, and intentional. Our mission is to help people form small, interest-aligned groups — “loops” — that lower planning friction, build trust, and turn new cities into communities.

We’re starting simple and community-first: transparent plans, respectful norms, and tools that keep logistics light. As we grow, we’re adding richer profiles, trust features, and curated loops in top destinations.

How Our Matching Works (Today)

We compute a simple compatibility score between your saved preferences and each loop’s preferences:

  • Exact matches: +3 points each for climate, pace, and budget.
  • Activities overlap: up to +6 points using Jaccard overlap (shared ÷ union).
  • Total score ≈ 0–15. Higher = better fit.
