About LoopMeets

LoopMeets is a traveler-matching platform designed to make solo travel social, safe, and intentional. Our mission is to help people form small, interest-aligned groups — “loops” — that lower planning friction, build trust, and turn new cities into communities.

Why LoopMeets Exists

Traveling solo can be freeing — and also lonely. LoopMeets makes it easy to find the right people for the right moments: a sunrise hike, a ramen crawl, a museum afternoon. Small groups, shared interests, clear expectations.

The Inspiration

From chance encounters to intentional community

LoopMeets grew from countless conversations with travelers who wanted spontaneous plans without the chaos of giant group chats. We focused on signal over noise: preferences that matter and logistics that stay light.

Founder: Roshni Anarkat

Roshni is passionate about community design and accessible travel. Her goal with LoopMeets: help travelers feel safe, welcome, and excited to say “yes” to exploring together.

How matching works (now)

We compute a compatibility score between your saved preferences and each loop’s preferences:

  • Exact matches: +3 each for climate, pace, budget.
  • Activities overlap: up to +6 via Jaccard (shared ÷ union).
  • Total: ~0–15, higher = better fit.

What’s next (roadmap)

  • Trust & safety signals (mutuals, attendance streaks).
  • Location/time-aware suggestions.
  • Weighted interests & learning-based recommendations.

We’ll evolve transparently, with user control at the center.
